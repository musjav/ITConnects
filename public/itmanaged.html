IT Managed Services

As global IT dynamics are shifting, onus and priorities of IT Executives are also calibrating towards more pressing issues. We believe that CIOs & IT Managers need to render additional focus on IT planning budgeting, forecasting, learning etc. rather than mismanaging time on deployments, daily routine IT operations or on other HR related issues.

We discern that a gap exist within market in providing quality services according to organization size, business nature and requirements. There are only handful IT Companies who have the capacities to render comprehensive & holistic services solutions.

ITConnects is a full IT service provider entity that not only recognize market demands but also distinguish prerequisite of available technical resources.

To overcome all related issues, we developed a set of IT Managed Service plans that encompass every solution required by either small, medium or else conglomerate enterprises.




IT Managed Services (IMS) are a dedicated services which can be tailored as per your IT needs. It’s an specially designed services for a companies that;

  • Don’t have a dedicated in-house IT department and not willing to hire any permanent resource to manage the IT operations or any particular area, so they can minimize the cost and other HR related issues.
  • Have IT resources or department but need extra assistance/support to manage the overall IT operations or any particular area.
    • Through these services, any organization can outsource its complete IT operations or any particular area to ITConnects for management and monitoring or they can take human resources from us on a short or long term contract to manage complete IT Operations or any particular operation.
